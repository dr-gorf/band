Hello!

We're a band named Dr. GORF. We've been making music under various titles for over 10 years

At its heart, Dr. GORF is just a couple of washed-up has-beens trying to re-live their glory days by recording ironically absurd music. But if you look past that, you'll find a beautiful tapestry of genre-transcending sonic poetry.

From the distorted grungy punk stylings of "pFixie" to the explicit Gangsta-rap lyrics of "Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer" - Dr. GORF has done it all, and will continue to do it all long after people lose interest.
