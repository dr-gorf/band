Hello

We're Supple Congress and we've been making music together for over 10 years

At its heart, Supple Congress is just a couple of has-beens trying to relive their glory days by recording ironically absurd music. But if you look past that, you find a rich tapestry of genre-transcending sonic poetry.

Our style knows no bounds, and our standards have never been lower. From the grungy punk guitars in "pFixie" to the explicit Gangsta-rap lyrics of "Rudolph the Red-Nosed Reindeer" - Supple Congress has done it all, and will continue to do it all long after people lose interest.
